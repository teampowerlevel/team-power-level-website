Team Power Level

Team Power Level is a collection of 10,000 NFT trading cards built on the Ethereum blockchain. Our trading cards parody animation and underground culture icons from the late 80s to the early 2000s. Ownership of a Team Power Level trading card doubles as your membership to Team Power Level. Members-only perks can be unlocked through roadmap activation.

The Specs

Team Power Level trading cards are all unique and are programmatically generated from over 420 possible attributes including eyes, mouths, ears, weapons, and more.

Each NFT trading card is stored as an ERC-721 token on the Ethereum blockchain. Before the full reveal, each trading card will be hosted on a centralized server to prevent 'sniping' for rarer cards and to keep a fair playing field for all. Once all 10,000 cards have been minted and revealed, the data will be reverted back to IPFS where it will be locked for good.
